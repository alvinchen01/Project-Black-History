Everyday Black Resilience

The Story of Ruby Bridges and Ongoing Strength

Who Was Ruby Bridges?

Ruby Bridges was six years old when she became the first Black child to attend an all white elementary school in New Orleans in 1960. She faced protests, threats, and isolation, but she continued going to school every single day. Her courage showed that even a child can stand strong in the face of injustice.

Why This Matters
